Functions like sbrk, write, and fstat are in libcf.a.  You might want to 
check that linkfile.ld contains:

   GROUP(-lc -lunhosted -lcf)

See D:\CS\m68k-elf/lib/m5208/m5208evb-rom.ld for an example.
