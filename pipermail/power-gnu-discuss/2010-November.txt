CodeSourcery is pleased to announce the availability of the 2010.09
Sourcery G++ Lite Edition for PowerPC processors.  This release is
available for download from:

    http://www.codesourcery.com/sgpp/lite/powerpc

Some of the new features in this release include:

* Updated GCC to version 4.5.1, and updated versions of binutils and
  GDB.

* The sqrt and sqrtf routines in libm have been optimized.

* For processors that support the isel instruction, a new __builtin_isel
  intrinsic has been added.

* And of course, many bug fixes.
