Hi,

As far as I know, the correct syntax would be more like
addu $0, $1, $2

You can shortcut by defining macros like:
#define a0      $4
#define a1      $5
#define a2      $6
#define a3      $7

which yields

addu a0, a1, a3

Don't know what the assembler interprets if you mix letters in there.

JB
